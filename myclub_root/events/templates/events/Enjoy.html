What a year this has been!

  It started amazing, moved to Selly with few of my friends, some late-night parties and great memories. Then the assignments started, those weren't that bad, to be honest, but you could feel the weight of the second year coming in.
  What I've enjoyed was the fact that our coursework was focused more on team-work and less on individual activities. I usually tend to tackle the challenges and requirements easier when being part of a team.
  Therefore, the software engineer module was my favourite one so far. My team worked marvelous. I was lucky to be with them, everyone worked as hard as they possibly could and, in the end, we managed to 'deliver' a great app.
  During the second term, things were a bit different, but I think it was the first time I went through so many things at the same time. Found Security and Mathematical Modelling challenging and highly time-consuming. What really saved me was the Team Project. If during the first term everything we've worked on was kind of hypothetical, now we had to create, develop, integrate, re-think, reorder and deliver a complete and unique game.
